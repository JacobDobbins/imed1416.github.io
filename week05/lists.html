Tapenade (Olive Spread)

This is a really simple dish to prepare and it's always a big hit at parties. My father recomends:

"Make this the night before so that the flavors time to blend. Just bring it up to room temputure before you serve it. In the winter, try serving it warm"

Ingredients

  • 1 8oz. jar sundried tomatoes
  • 2 large garlic cloves
  • 2/3 c. kalamata olives
  • 1 t. capers

Instructions

  1. Combine tomatoes and garlic in a food processor. Blend untill as smooth as possi-ble.
  2. Add capers and olives. Pulse themotor a few times until they are incorporated, but still retain some texture
Serve on thin rounds with goat cheese and fresh basil garnish (optional).
